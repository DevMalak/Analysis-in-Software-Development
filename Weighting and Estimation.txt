Weighting and Estimation 

Story Point Estimation Story points measure relative effort based on complexity, risk, and uncertainty. 

Techniques 

Planning Poker: Team votes using Fibonacci numbers. 

T-Shirt Sizing: XS, S, M, L, XL for rough effort. 

Dot Voting: Quick prioritization by team consensus. 

Weighting User Stories 

Complexity: Technical difficulty. 

Priority: Business urgency. 

Business Value: ROI or customer impact.
