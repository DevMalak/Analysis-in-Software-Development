 Product Backlog Development 

Definition & Purpose A Product Backlog is a dynamic, ordered list of features, enhancements, and fixes that represent everything needed in the product. Purpose: Serves as the single source of truth for development work. 

Creation & Structure 

Items: Features, bugs, technical tasks, research spikes. 

Format: User stories, epics, tasks. 

Organized by priority and business value.
